We have practices in place to protect your privacy. In the future, we will consider technical solutions including encryption.

At the current stage of our product development, we do not implement encryption. However, from the perspective of what we can easily control, we take steps to help you be able to use our service with reassurance.

  • We do not retain information from you by default. You will be able to provide information for us to save on an active consent basis.
  • In the future, we plan to implement end-to-end encryption to increase user security confidence.
  • Our staff are bound by our guidelines to not disclose details of conversations.
